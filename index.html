Bulk Manufacturer of Expeller Coconut Oil

Aarudhra Oil Industries is a bulk manufacturer and supplier of expeller-pressed coconut oil, catering to traders and industrial buyers across India.

Located in Kangayam, Tamil Nadu — a region known for coconut cultivation and oil milling — our operations focus on consistent quality, reliable sourcing, and long-term supply relationships.

We follow traditional expeller processes combined with strict quality checks to ensure our products meet the requirements of bulk and industrial use.

  • Bulk supply for industrial and trading use
  • Consistent quality and reliable sourcing
  • South Indian coconuts
